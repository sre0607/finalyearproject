Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does same-day delivery work?
A: For orders placed before 1 PM local time, we offer direct doorstep delivery by that evening. Orders placed after 1 PM will be scheduled for next-day delivery.
Q: Can I schedule a delivery for a future date?
A: Yes! During the checkout flow, you can specify delivery details. For advanced customizations or exact delivery timings (e.g. morning surprise), please reach out via our Help & Support form.
Q: How do I keep my flowers fresh?
A: We guarantee stem freshness for at least 7 days. To maximize vase life, cut stems diagonally at a 45-degree angle under water, use clean tap water, avoid direct heat/drafts, and replace water every 2 days.
Q: What is your return or refund policy?
A: Since flowers are perishable, we do not accept returns. However, if your blooms arrive damaged or fail to meet our freshness guarantee within 7 days, send us a photo via our Help center, and we will issue a replacement or full refund.
Q: Do you offer bulk discounts or corporate arrangements?
A: Yes! We craft customized wedding setups and corporate office subscriptions. Drop us an inquiry using the Contact Us form, and our lead florist will reach out within 24 hours.
